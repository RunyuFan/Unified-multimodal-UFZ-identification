BOVW.py: extracts visual features of street view images and forms visual word frequency vectors.
image_captioning.py: obtains text descriptions of street view images.
doc_embeddings.py & graph_embeddings.py: extract features of the street view text.
dataset.py: model input dataset format.
kan.py: KAN model file.
model.py: the proposed model.
train.py, val.py, pre.py: trains the model, verifies the model accuracy, and predicts the functional category of the plot based on the model.
